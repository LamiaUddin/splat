Information Required To Complete Your Application(s)

Your school will send your Grade 11 and 12 results to us through the OUAC. Do not submit report cards or your Ontario Secondary School transcript unless requested; if these items were not specifically requested they will not be reviewed. Check your OUAC application to ensure your school has reported your academic record correctly. Any additional documents required to complete your application are indicated below. If there are no required documents listed below then all required information is being provided by your school.

Document Deadline: February 15 is the deadline for receipt of English facility test results or transcripts, if you have been requested to provide this information. As many offers of admission will be made in the earlier admission rounds, and space is limited, meeting the deadline ensures that your documents are available for this early consideration. Late documents will be considered as space allows. The deadline dates for any non-academic documentation, such as a supplementary application, will be specified by the division which requires them.

Submitting Required Documents

  • Documents must be uploaded or sent electronically (e.g. Parchment/Naviance, OUAC electronic submissions, etc.)
  • AP, SAT, ACT, IB, TOEFL, IELTS, and COPE/ELDA test scores must be sent electronically. Uploaded test scores will not be accepted.
  • If you are unable to upload or electronically submit your documents, you must arrange for your institution to send your documents directly to Enrolment Services. Note: Your documents will not be added to your application if the name on your documents is not identical to the name on your OUAC application. You must also include your U of T Applicant number or OUAC reference number.
  • We do not retain documents from applications in previous years. Transcripts received by the University of Toronto become the property of the University and will not be returned or forwarded to other institutions.
